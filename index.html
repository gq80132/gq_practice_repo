For the second week in a row Rafael Navarro has been named to MLS's Team of the Matchday for his skill in front of goal, this time for notching the Rapids' lone goal in Austin to propel his team to its first three-point performance of the season. The Brazilian striker made the difference for Colorado in the 18th minute at Q2 Stadium, as midfielder Djordje Mihailovic split the Austin backline for Navarro's run in behind to face goalkeeper Brad Stuver one on one. His chip over Stuver floated into the back of the net for the only goal of the match.
